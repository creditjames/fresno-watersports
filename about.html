Our Story

From Lake Days to a Lifestyle

Fresno Watersports was born from one simple idea — everyone deserves an incredible day on the water. Growing up in the Central Valley, we spent every summer at the lake. We knew the joy of ripping across open water, the peace of a sunset cruise, and the laughs that come with a day spent with friends and family.

We started this company to share that feeling with everyone. Whether you're a first-time rider or a seasoned boater, we make it easy, affordable, and unforgettable. Our fleet is meticulously maintained, our team genuinely cares about your experience, and we deliver directly to the lake so you can focus on one thing — having fun.
