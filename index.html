Where We Ride

Our Lakes

From calm morning waters to wide-open afternoon runs — we bring the fun to four of the best lakes in Central California.

Millerton Lake

Fresno's backyard lake — warm water, big coves, and perfect for a full day of boating and riding.

Pine Flat Lake

Crystal-clear water nestled in the foothills. Great for families and thrill-seekers alike.

Shaver Lake

A mountain escape at 5,370 ft. Pine-lined shores and cool alpine water — pure paradise.

Bass Lake

The crown jewel of the Sierra. Stunning scenery, warm summers, and endless water fun.

Common Questions

In California, anyone born on or after January 1, 1986 needs a California Boater Card to operate a motorized vessel. For jet skis, you must be at least 16 years old. We'll go over all safety requirements at pickup.

Bring sunscreen, towels, water, snacks, and a valid photo ID. We provide life jackets for all passengers. We recommend wearing clothes you don't mind getting wet!

Yes, a refundable security deposit is required at the time of rental. The amount varies by watercraft type. The deposit is fully refunded upon safe return of the equipment.

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your reservation. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a fee. Weather-related cancellations are always fully refunded.
